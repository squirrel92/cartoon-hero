Minion Species

Origins

Minions are small, yellow creatures who have existed since the beginning of time, evolving from yellow single-celled organisms into beings which exist only to serve history's most evil masters.

Why were they in isolation?

After rolling a Tyrannosaurus into a volcano, getting their caveman leader eaten by a bear, crushing a Pharaoh under a pyramid after constructing it upside-down, exposing Count Dracula to sunlight, and accidentally firing a cannon at Napoleon while in Russia, the Minions are driven into isolation and start a new life in a massive cave.

  1. After many years, the Minions become depressed and unmotivated without a master to serve.
  2. To regain their dignity and sense of purpose, Kevin recruits Stuart and Bob, setting out to look for a new master.
